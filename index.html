§ I · Selected Engagement

Cloud modernization
for Latin America's
largest independent
retailer.

The Client

One of Latin America's most enduring independent supermarket chains, operating more than two hundred retail locations across thirteen provinces. The organization's omnichannel platform serves millions of monthly users across grocery and electronics verticals, with significant traffic concentrations during national commerce events.

The Context

The ecommerce platform operated on twenty-one EC2 instances with manual scaling procedures. Infrastructure expenditure had grown untenable, peak events required all-hands operational rituals, and the deployment pipeline had become a quarterly source of institutional risk. Leadership sought comprehensive counsel on modernization strategy and execution.

“

The engagement transformed twenty-one hand-provisioned instances into a single auto-scaling cluster with intelligent node provisioning — ninety percent spot ratio, fully automated for peak-load events, zero manual intervention required.

— Engagement Summary, MMXXV

§ IV · Engagement Model

A deliberate,
disciplined
approach.

  1. I / Introduction

    A private conversation.

    A thirty-minute introduction with a founding principal. We assess your technical context, objectives, and constraints — and determine together whether Webytex is the appropriate practice for your engagement.

  2. II / Engagement Brief

    A written proposal.

    A concise engagement brief within one business week: scope, team composition, technical approach, timeline, and fee arrangement. No lengthy decks — if we cannot articulate the engagement in two pages, we have not yet understood it.

  3. III / Commencement

    Integrated delivery.

    Our team joins your collaboration tools, source control, and planning cadence within one business week of commencement. No prolonged onboarding, no discovery extensions — delivery begins immediately.

  4. IV / Execution

    Weekly cadence.

    Transparent weekly progress reviews, production-grade deliverables, and comprehensive documentation. At engagement close, your organization retains full ownership of the codebase, architectural decisions, and institutional knowledge.
